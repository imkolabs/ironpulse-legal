Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the IronPulse app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Imko Labs (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Freemium service.

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (IP address)
  • The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit
  • The time spent on those pages
  • The operating system you use on your mobile device

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address - Used for authentication and account recovery
  • Display name - Used to personalize your experience
  • Profile photo - Optional, for your profile

Workout Data

To provide our core service, we collect and store:

  • Workout sessions and exercise logs
  • Sets, reps, and weight information
  • Personal records and progress data
  • Workout schedules and preferences
  • Body measurements (if provided)
Your Data Ownership: All workout data you enter belongs to you. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings.

Third-Party Services

The Application uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

Google Services

  • Firebase Authentication - For secure sign-in and account management
  • Firebase Firestore - For storing your workout data securely in the cloud
  • Google Sign-In - Optional authentication method

Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect:

  • Transaction identifiers
  • Subscription status
  • Device identifiers for fraud prevention

RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Google Gemini AI

Our AI Coach feature uses Google's Gemini API. When using this feature:

  • Your messages are processed by Google's AI service
  • Conversation context may include your workout data to provide personalized advice

Google AI privacy: https://ai.google/responsibility/principles/

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase's infrastructure with:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest
  • Secure authentication tokens
  • Regular security updates

Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all collection of information by the Application by uninstalling it. You may use the standard uninstall processes available on your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you use the Application and have an account. If you delete your account:

  • Your personal information is deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained for improvement purposes

Children's Privacy

The Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification - Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure - Request deletion of your data
  • Portability - Request export of your data
